  • Numbers and operands appear on the white screen on clicking the values.
  • After writing the operation, click = to evaluate.
  • This calculator can perform one operation at a time. Multiple operations not allowed.
  • This calculator cannot take negative values. ( Eg: 3√-96 will give error )
  • For finding ln first click operand and then provide the number. ( Eg : ln 456 )
  • For finding sin, cos, tan, first click operand and then provide value in degrees (Radians are not allowed).
  • For finding nth root, lognx, xn, first click on the operand, then provide value and then click on n to give value of n.
